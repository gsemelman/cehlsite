CEHL Coaches Contracts

(Updated Tues. May 29th, 2018)

Please note that CEHL rules state that all coaches must be bought out of their current contract, in order to be fired.

If you coach has one year remaining on his contract, an extension must be negotiated BEFORE the trade deadline or he will test the market following the playoffs.

Ryan Williams is the agent for all coaches contracts
