SEASON 1 PREVIEW
Central Division
DETROIT RED WINGS-  One of the best teams in the league, the Red Wings had to trade away a few of their guns to lower salary.  Out went Shanahan, Chelios, and Robitaille.  In came younger players such as, Stajan, Yonkman, Modin, Novak.  This team still looks very powerful.  They are lead by Yzerman, Federov, Hull, Lidstrom and Joseph, which is a very good core to make a cup run.  They also have solid young players in Modin, Zetterberg, and Williams.  This team may have traded away some big names, but overall they are still a very good team, and should be favoured to win their division.
TOTAL POINTS PREDICTION: 104
ST. LOUIS BLUES-  The Blues are a team with the potential to be successful.  Up front they have a potent offense that is led by Doug Weight and Pavol Demitra.  Their defense is solid with Pronger and Macinnis.  The big question the Blues need to answer is their goaltending.  Brent Johnson and Fred Brathwaite will fight it out for the number one job, but can either one of them lead a team very far?  The Blues are still 2 million dollars over the salary cap and could trade a big name to get a proven goalie.
TOTAL POINTS PREDICTION: 91
CHICAGO BLACKHAWKS-  The Blackhawks could be a surprise team in the west.  Up front they have names like, Zhamnov, Bell, Calder, Berezin, Daze, Sullivan, Thomas and Fleury which all comes together for what could be a very surprising overall offense.  The Blackhawks defense is their Achilles heal, and could end up being their downfall.  Housley is the proven defenseman, but he is getting on in age.  Other than him, the Hawks have Karpovtsev, Dempsey, and Odelin to make up their top 4.  For them to be a contender they must make a move to strengthen the defense.  In net Jocelyn Thibault is still a young goalie and for Chicago to be a contender, he must be able to steal some wins.
TOTAL POINTS PREDICTION: 86
COLUMBUS BLUE JACKETS- The Jackets are a rebuilding team.  They are not at the same level as the previous three teams in their division, and it would be a surprise if they made the playoffs.  They do have a bright future.  With players like Nash, Mcleod, Klesla, Grand-Pierre, and Denis the team is looking good in a few years.   For now they need to count on guys like, Whitney, Knutsen and Sanderson to carry the load and guide the younger players.  As for now, do not expect much form the Jackets, other than a good draft pick.
TOTAL POINTS PREDICTION: 60. 
NASHVILLE PREDATORS- Nashville has a way to go.  Led by Hartnell, Yachmenev, Timonen and youngster Legwand.  In net, the Preds recently traded for veteran goalie Mike Richter.  Don't count them out, but the Preds do need a lot of things to go their way if they want to make a playoff run
TOTAL POINTS PREDICTION: 55
