The regular season is over so it’s time for GMs to vote for the CEHL season 3 awards.  Each team gets one vote per award and are allowed to vote for their own players.  Alond with voting for each of the awards, please select 2 centers, 2 LWs, 2 RWs, 4 d-men and 2 goalies for the CEHL 1st & 2nd all-star teams ((you don’t have to indicate whether you are voting them on the 1st or 2nd team.)) I will accept ballots until the start of the playoffs (Tuesday night). Also, please note: IF YOU DON’T VOTE, YOUR PLAYERS WON’T WIN AWARDS
