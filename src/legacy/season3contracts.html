CEHL Contracts (Updated Wed. Jan. 19, 2004)

The minimum CEHL salary is $300,000

SB - Signing Bonus (may not exceed three times the base salary, i.e. the max SB on a $1 mil base salary is $3 mil)

PB - Performance Bonus (starting in season 3, may not exceed the base salary, i.e. max PBs on a $1 mil contract  is $1 mil per  season)

* - denotes contract extensions effective end of season 3
