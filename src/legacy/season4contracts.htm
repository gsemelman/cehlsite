CEHL Contracts (Season 4 Contracts)

The minimum CEHL salary is $300,000

SB - Signing Bonus (may not exceed three times the base salary, i.e. the max SB on a $1 mil base salary is $3 mil)

PB - Performance Bonus (may not exceed the base salary, i.e. max PBs on a $1 mil contract  is $1 mil per  season)

* - denotes contract effective end of season 4
